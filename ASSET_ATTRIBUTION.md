# Audio Asset Attribution

This file tracks all audio assets used in Williams World, including their sources, authors, licenses, and URLs.

## License Requirements
- All assets must have clear, permissive licenses
- CC0 (Public Domain) is preferred
- CC-BY requires attribution in the Credits page
- Avoid NC (Non-Commercial) licenses
- Avoid unclear licensing or ripped game audio
