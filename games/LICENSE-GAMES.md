## Privacy & Safety

All games in this directory:
- ✅ Are fully self-hosted with no external dependencies
- ✅ Use only localStorage for score/state persistence
- ✅ Make NO external network requests
- ✅ Collect NO personal data
- ✅ Are kid-safe and appropriate for a 10-year-old
- ✅ Use only permissive open-source licenses (MIT)
